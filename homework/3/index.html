Part 1: Student grade calculator that uses a form with input boxes for Homework Average, Mid-Term Exam Score, Final Exam Score, and Participation.The form has buttons to submit the data and clear the form. There is a textarea that presents final results and/or an error message.

Part 2: Seller weekly earning's calculator. A company pays its salespeople on a commission basis. The salespeople receive $250 per week, plus 9% of their gross sales for the week. jQuery UI tooltip is used to display a message when the user's mouse hover's over the Seller Name field. The user inputs the quantity of items sold. The results of the total sold and total money that will be received by the salesperson is displayed and right justified.
